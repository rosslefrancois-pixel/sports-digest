The Bruins traded their 2026 first-round pick (No. 23) and Florida's 2028 first-rounder to Utah for winger JJ Peterka, 24, who is signed through 2029–30 at $7.7M AAV.
Peterka scored 25 goals in 82 games for Utah last season after posting 68 points in his final year in Buffalo; Boston hopes a change of scenery unlocks his upside.
In the draft's Day 2, Boston also selected G Yuri Ivanov (No. 56, Rd. 2, MHK Spartak Moskva) and six more prospects, and traded prospect Fabian Lysell to Colorado for prospect Ivan Ivan.
Bruins UFA targets entering July 1: D Rasmus Andersson (Vegas) has been linked to Boston — Sportsnet reported the club was 'in the driver's seat' in earlier trade talks.
The 2026–27 preseason opens Sept. 20 vs. Washington at TD Garden (4-game slate); regular-season schedule not yet announced — rumored regular-season opener around Sept. 29.
David Pastrnak posted a 100-point regular season (29 G, 41 A); goalie Jeremy Swayman was the Bruins' standout in the playoff loss to Buffalo.
